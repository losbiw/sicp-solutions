## Before modification

```scheme
(magnitude z)
(magnitude ('complex 'rectangular 3 4))
(apply-generic 'magnitude ('complex 'rectangular 3 4))
```

apply-generic looks for a magnitude procedure type-tagged as complex, which does not exist.

## After modification

```scheme
(magnitude z)
(magnitude ('complex 'rectangular 3 4))
(apply-generic 'magnitude ('complex 'rectangular 3 4))
(apply magnitude (('rectangular 3 4)))
```

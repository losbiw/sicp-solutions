```scheme
(define (gcd a b)
	(if (= b 0)
	a
	(gcd b (remainder a b)))
```

**Reduce to primitives first**

### Applicative order

**Evaluate first**

(gcd 206 40)
(gcd 40 (remainder 206 40)) -> (gcd 40 6)
(gcd 6 (remainder 40 6)) -> (gcd  6 4)
(gcd 4 (remainder 6 4)) -> (gcd 4 2)
(gcd 2 (remainder 4 2)) -> (gcd 2 0)
